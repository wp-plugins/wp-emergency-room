== Description ==

Having problems with your site ? Do you have a WordPress issue/error or task that you need help with ? WP Emergency Room (E.R) can help !

WP Emergency Room makes it easy for any webmaster or site owner to get help from curated WordPress support experts (directly from your dashboard) - for all your WP related fixes and tasks. Simply click the non-obtrusive WPER HELP button (seen in the top right of your dashboard), and ask us for help. This button will be accessible throughout your admin dashboard (all pages) so help is always just one click away. Support at your fingertips.

**Features**

* One click support directly from your WordPress dashboard
* Compatible with multi-site setups (networks) and WooCommerce 
* Support button is non-obtrusive and easily accessible throughout your admin

The WP Emergency Room (E.R) plugin is maintained by [YOOPlugins.com](http://yooplugins.com/)

== Installation ==

1. upload '/nwpemergency911/` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. the WPER HELP button will now appear in the top right corner of your admin dashboard

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Does this plugin work on multi-site setups = 
Absolutely! Our plugin is network compatible and can be used on multi-site setups (giving all your network users easy access to our support channel with ease)

= Is there any charge to use the WP Emergency Room service ? = 
Yes! Unfortunately there is. However, we are the most affordable support service in the field. Single fixes/tasks are charged at only $15 once-off. We also have a 10 Credits package available which gives you 10 x fixes/tasks which you can use whenever and however you need them.

= How long does it take for you to complete a fix/task ? =
We complete tasks on the same business day, most within 6 hours of receiving the ticket. However, please understand that we do not provide full guarantee either express or implied on the timeframe of when a support request can or will be resolved. If you request a task over the weekend, we generally take care of it on Monday morning unless we deem it to be urgent.

= I just submitted my support request. When will you start fixing my issue? =
The WP Emergency Room team will start working on solving your problem on the same business day you sign up and submit your request.

= Are there limits to the tasks WP Emergency Room will do ? =
To help make sure we can keep all of our customers happy, we only do small fixes and tasks. This means that 60 minute tasks are in scope and larger, more complex tasks, exceeding this frame, are out of scope.

= Do you offer a money back guarantee ? =
Yes - We do!

= Is WP Emergency Room safe to use ? =
Absolutely! We take your security and privacy seriously and carefully safegaurd your personal details, login credentials and other pieces of information. Furthermore, all tasks are done from highly secured setups.
